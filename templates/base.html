What Is Unified Attendance System?

Unified Attendance System is a smart, role-aware platform designed to help schools and institutions track attendance with speed, security, and clarity. It replaces manual logs with QR-based check-ins, personalized dashboards, and real-time analytics — all optimized for mobile.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Admin registers and sets up departments.
  • Step 2: Teachers and secretaries register under their assigned roles.
  • Step 3: Users scan QR codes via camera or file upload to log attendance.
  • Step 4: Dashboards display attendance logs, charts, and export options.
